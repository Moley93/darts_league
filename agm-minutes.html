Approval of the Minutes of the AGM of 22nd August 2023

Agreed as a true record. No matters arising.

Proposed by Tom White, Seconded by Tracey Jenner

Chairman's Report

Mick welcomed everyone to the meeting and asked all to raise a glass in remembrance of members we have sadly lost this year.

Mick thanked all teams for their participation last season and especially to all who came to support the charity night. One of the charities we raised money for actually attended the event and were hugely grateful. Mick thanked Claire Howes for the amazing work she puts in to gain raffle prizes.

Mick thanked Clare Sabourn and Jackie Moran for their work as Secretary and Treasurer and all of the committee members.

Mick wished everyone good luck for the coming season.

Secretary's Report

Clare thanked everyone who attended or contributed to the charity night which has now been running for 3 years. The walk on music was a great addition this year!

Clare thanked Claire Howes again for her hard work in securing raffle prizes. Special thanks as well to Claire and Jennie Duggan who were always selling raffle tickets and to all teams who sold raffle tickets at their venues.

There was a good turnout at finals night which was enjoyed by all who attended. Clare thanked Tracey Jenner, Mick Kirrage and Bernadette Grantham who provided food on the night.

Rules

  • Rule 12: It was agreed that if a team had a minimum of 3 players in attendance, the match would be played. Less than 3 will be a no show.
  • Nearest the bull to start: This was not agreed. Home team to throw first.
  • Start time: Start time will remain at 8.30pm. Captains may agree to start earlier.
  • Players names to be drawn for singles: This rule remains unchanged.
  • Trebles competition: To be discussed in AOB.
  • Singles draw: Only names of players in attendance at the time of the draw may be in the draw.
  • Bonus points for a win: Not agreed.
  • Return of runners-up trophies: This was changed last year due to medals being left behind after finals night. It was agreed that runners-up will receive medals again.

AOB
